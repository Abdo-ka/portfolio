## About

[CKP](https://www.ckpartners.com.my/) Portal is a B2B invoicing and accounting platform that automates invoice creation, delivery, and tracking between businesses and their suppliers/customers.

I led the development team and worked on the frontend.

### Tech Stack

**Frontend**: React.js, Tanstack Router, Tanstack Query, Shadcn UI.

**Backend**: NestJS, PostgreSQL, Kysely.
