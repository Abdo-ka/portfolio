## About

A religious App amassed a user base of over **1M** on Google Play. It provides users with a wide range of religious content, including Quran readings, Hadith collections, prayer times, and more. The app is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, making it easy for users to engage with their faith on a daily basis. 

### Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Flutter, Dart.
- **Backend**: Laravel.
- **Deployment**: Google Play, App Store, Firebase Analytics.
